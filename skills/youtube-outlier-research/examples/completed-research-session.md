## Top Insights from This Session

1. **Specificity wins**: Videos with specific numbers (80%, 20 hours, etc.) outperform vague claims
2. **Objection-busting is powerful**: "You don't need to be technical" addresses the #1 barrier for our ICP
3. **Before/after hooks dominate**: Personal transformation stories resonate more than abstract teaching
4. **8-12 minutes is the sweet spot**: Long enough to provide value, short enough to maintain attention
5. **Face + emotion thumbnails crush it**: 62.5% of outliers use this format

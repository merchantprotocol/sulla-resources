# YouTube Outlier Research Skill

This skill folder contains the complete **Hookpoint Method** for YouTube video research — a systematic process to find, analyze, and replicate winning video formats from outlier videos.

## What's Inside

### Core Documentation

- **`SKILL.md`** — Complete methodology and step-by-step process
  - The full Hookpoint research framework
  - How to find outlier videos
  - How to analyze patterns (title, thumbnail, hook, structure)
  - How to synthesize findings
  - How to generate and score video ideas
  - Weekly research routine

### Templates

- **`outlier-tracking-sheet.md`** — Track individual outlier videos with detailed analysis fields
- **`pattern-synthesis-template.md`** — Synthesize patterns across multiple videos
- **`video-idea-scorecard.md`** — Score and prioritize video ideas based on pattern matching
- **`content-brief-template.md`** — Create production-ready briefs for approved videos

### Examples

- **`examples/completed-research-session.md`** — Full example of a completed research session with 8 outlier videos analyzed, patterns synthesized, and video ideas generated

## Quick Start

1. **Read `SKILL.md`** — Understand the full methodology
2. **Open `outlier-tracking-sheet.md`** — Start your first research session
3. **Find 10-20 outlier videos** — Use the search strategies in SKILL.md
4. **Analyze each video** — Fill out the tracking sheet for each
5. **Synthesize patterns** — Use `pattern-synthesis-template.md`
6. **Generate ideas** — Score ideas with `video-idea-scorecard.md`
7. **Create briefs** — Use `content-brief-template.md` for top ideas

## Time Commitment

- **Initial research session**: 2-3 hours
- **Weekly maintenance**: 2-3 hours (Monday/Wednesday/Friday routine)
- **Monthly review**: 2 hours

## Expected Output

Per research session:
- 10-20 outlier videos identified and analyzed
- 5-7 golden patterns synthesized
- 10-20 video ideas generated
- 3-5 high-scoring ideas ready for production
